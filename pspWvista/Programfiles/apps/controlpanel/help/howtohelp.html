How to use this help

At the bottom of any page, you have two related links, for read more interesting things of pspwvista or for obtain more help.

For navigate in the help, put your mouse on this page and push [L] trigger for go back or [R] trigger for go next.

For scrolling the page, put your mouse on this page, hold square and move the analog stick.
