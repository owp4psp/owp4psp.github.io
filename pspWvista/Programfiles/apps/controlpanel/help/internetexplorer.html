Internet Explorer

Internet Explorer is the web browser of pspwvista. It is made for seems to Windows Internet Exlorer. You can acess to internet explorer by clicking on the desktop icon, in all programs/Internet, or clicking in any link of links section.

The interface of Internet Explorer (it can change in future versions).

To go to any web page, type in the URL box at the top an click Go. Or open your bookmarks and click on any link.

For scroll, hold square and move the analog stick.

Buttons of Internet Explorer navbar:

Allows to back to previous page (the same function of L trigger)
Allows to go to later page (the same funcion of R trigger)
With this button you can view the PSP internet browser bookmarks.
It refresh the actually page.
Allows to go to your home page (defined in user/browser.js).
