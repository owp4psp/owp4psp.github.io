Introduction to pspwvista

pspwvista allows you to use Windows Vista on your Playstation Portable. Well, really it is just a set of webpages that look similar to Windows Vista, but they are offline. It has some useful features: lots of games and applications. You do not need to access the internet for most of pspwvista, only to access the links and use the internet browser.

With pspwvista you have lots of games and aplications and you can add your own!

Requeriments

Firmware 2.7 or above
Adobe Flash® enabled
17 MB free space
